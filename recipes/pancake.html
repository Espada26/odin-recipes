Pancake Recipe

This easy pancake recipe doesn't require much thought early in the morning and the pancakes taste great!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk and oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. 1. Combine the dry ingredients.
  2. 2. Add the wet ingredients and mix.
  4. 3. Pour or ladle the batter onto the oiled griddle or pan.
  5. 4. Cook until bubbles from, flip, and cook in the other side.
